## Description: <br>
Helps agents create and maintain lightweight Architecture Decision Records that capture the context, rationale, and consequences of significant technical choices. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wpank](https://clawhub.ai/user/wpank) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineering teams use this skill to decide when an ADR is warranted, draft ADRs from reusable templates, maintain an ADR directory and index, and review architectural decisions before acceptance. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Installation or optional tooling guidance may modify the local environment or repository. <br>
Mitigation: Review the source and commands before installing, and use the skill only in repositories where ADR documentation changes are expected. <br>
Risk: Generated ADRs may record incomplete or misleading rationale for important technical decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Have affected teams and senior engineers review ADR context, options, consequences, and security implications before acceptance. <br>
