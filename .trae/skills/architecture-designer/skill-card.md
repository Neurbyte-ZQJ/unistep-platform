## Description: <br>
Helps developers and architects design or review system architectures, choose patterns, document ADRs, and plan scalability. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Developers, engineers, and architecture reviewers use this skill to gather requirements, compare architecture patterns, document decisions as ADRs, and produce architecture recommendations with trade-offs and risks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may activate on broad architecture-related wording or provide advice that does not fit the current project. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the skill's recommendations against the actual requirements, constraints, stakeholder input, security needs, cost limits, and operational context before adopting a design. <br>
Risk: Architecture recommendations can be incomplete if non-functional requirements or failure modes are not fully supplied. <br>
Mitigation: Use the included NFR checklist and require explicit review of scalability, performance, availability, security, reliability, maintainability, and cost before finalizing decisions. <br>
